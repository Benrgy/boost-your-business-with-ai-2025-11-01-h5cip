About AI Automation Bureau

Founded in 2020 by a team of former enterprise automation consultants and software engineers, AI Automation Bureau emerged from a simple observation: powerful AI and automation technology existed, but it remained inaccessible to small businesses and startups. Our founders spent years helping Fortune 500 companies implement complex automation solutions, only to realize that the same transformative technology could be simplified and democratized for smaller organizations. We decided to leave the corporate world and build a platform that brings enterprise-grade automation to businesses of all sizes, without the enterprise-grade complexity or price tag.

Today, we're proud to serve over 500 businesses across 15 countries, from scrappy startups to established SMBs. Our team has grown to include machine learning specialists, product designers, and customer success experts, all united by a single mission: making AI automation accessible to everyone.

Our mission is to empower small businesses with AI-driven automation that drives real, measurable results. We believe that technology should simplify work, not complicate it. We're committed to building products that are not only powerful but also intuitive, affordable, and backed by exceptional customer support. We measure our success not by the complexity of our technology, but by the tangible impact we have on our clients' businesses.

Our core values guide everything we do: Accessibility (technology should be for everyone), Simplicity (we eliminate unnecessary complexity), Transparency (we're honest about what our platform can and can't do), and Impact (we focus obsessively on delivering real business results). We're not just building software; we're building a movement to democratize automation and help small businesses compete on a level playing field with larger enterprises.
